# Project 1: Social Media Content Planning

## Brand
Brew Haven Café

## Target Audience
- University students (18–25)
- Young professionals (22–35)
- Coffee lovers
- Freelancers

## Content Ideas
1. Coffee of the Week
2. Behind-the-scenes coffee preparation
3. Customer testimonials
4. Coffee facts and tips
5. Discount offers
6. Polls and quizzes
7. Lifestyle posts

## Posting Schedule

| Day | Content |
|------|---------|
| Monday | Coffee of the Week |
| Tuesday | Behind-the-Scenes Video |
| Wednesday | Customer Testimonial |
| Thursday | Coffee Fact |
| Friday | Discount Offer |
| Saturday | Poll/Quiz |
| Sunday | Lifestyle Post |

## Conclusion
This social media content plan helps increase brand awareness, engagement, and customer loyalty.
